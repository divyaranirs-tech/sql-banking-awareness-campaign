# sql-banking-awareness-campaign
Objectives:

⦁	Analyse bank marketing campaign dataset
⦁	Gain insights regarding customer behaviour,driving factors for subscription
⦁	Overall effectiveness of the campaign


Tools: valentina studio


What i did:

⦁	wrote sql queries to analyze customer data
⦁	calculated subscription rate and customer distribution
⦁	grouped customers by age segments and identified target groups
⦁	calculated month-over -month change percentage in subscription


Key insights:

⦁	Overall subscription rate: 11.52%
⦁	Major target category: Retired customers
⦁	Successful month: may
⦁	Most unsuccessful month:December
⦁	Targeted age category: below 30 and above 50
